Bolognese Lasagna

Description

A delicious bolognese lasagna!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Microwave
  • 1 Microwave bolognese lasagna

Steps:

  1. Open the box of the microwave bolognese lasagna;
  2. Put the lasagna into a microwave for 15 minutes;
  3. Wait until cold;
  4. Eat the delicious bolognese lasagna with a knife and a fork!
